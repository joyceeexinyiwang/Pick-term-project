Hello! Welcome to Pick.

Pick is a web app for a group of people to rate/compare/choose things together. You can use it for shopping, picking a club apparel, choosing a restaurant, etc.

Simply copy the link for something you like, and upload it by clicking "add option," and you will see it on the page. You may choose from Amazon, Ebay, Etsy, Macy's, Nordstrom, Yelp, Tripadvisor...

You can add criteria and their "importance," which will be used to calculate the weighted average ratings. After you and other users rate the options based on the criteria, Pick will calculate the averages, and rearrange the items based on the average ratings.

You can also view all of the ratings in ChartView.

For each option, you can add "pro" and "con," which are some extra information to consider when rating.

After you and the others decide on one of the options, you can click on that option's "This is it!" button. After every logged-in users click on it, you will be directed to the result page. If you regret it, you can also go back and rate again. You can also reset everything and start new.

This app intends to save time and energy, coz it is usually hard for a big group of people to choose something together.
